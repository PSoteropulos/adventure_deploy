The sun has risen in the sky, and the fog has all but lifted. A hooded figure with a staff appears from behind a tree in the forest. It appears they are beckoning you towards them.

What will you do?!

You know EXACTLY how this goes, and you refuse to embrace death after escaping so much so recently. You run along the forest edge, away from the figure. While ominous, you do not sense malice from the mysterious hooded figure. Against your better judgement you hesitantly walk towards the hooded figure. You turn around and briskly walk back towards the tavern. Even if they remain hostile, there is a better chance of survival amongst the crowd.
