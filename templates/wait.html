You fell asleep huddled in to the embrace of gnarled oak roots. Your groggy brain can not decipher if the howls it perceives are a dream, a memory, or reality. Dawn has broken, but the fog persists.

What will you do?!

You wake and gather yourself quickly, carefully placing one foot in front of the other as you slowly trek in to the menacing forest. You stay sitting at the base of a burled oak tree, hoping the mid day sun with bring a clear path and some warmth. You ponder the nature of memory, and decide to saunter towards the tavern. Surely the elapsed time has eased any tensions.
