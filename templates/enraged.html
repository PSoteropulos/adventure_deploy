The barmaid is visibly shaking with rage. She jumps on to the bar whilst letting out what can only be described as a roar. The ground below you trembles. This is very, very bad.

What will you do?!

You are not easily intimidated. You stand your ground. You feel a massive sausage of a finger tap your shoulder, and begin to turn around. You realize you have made many terrible choices recently, and you finally understand you need to escape immediately. You carefully calculate a clear and unobstructed path to the door, and dash like a shadow.
