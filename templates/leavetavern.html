You are immediately pierced to the bone by the dark, wet cold. The fog envelopes your vision as an icy rain pelts your skull. This is not a night to be adventuring.

What will you do?!

You reluctantly head towards a faintly glowing light near the forest's edge. You know this is does not bode well, and having been sobered by reality, ready the dagger you have sheathed in your boot. You "borrow" the lantern hanging outside the tavern, and stumble down the cobblestone road.
